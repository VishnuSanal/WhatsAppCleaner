Cleaner for WhatsApp - Clean Redundant Media and Files from Storage

Did you know that WhatsApp is cluttering your phone with unnecessary media, old
files, and duplicate images?! WhatsApp Cleaner can help you quickly clean up
your WhatsApp storage. With a simple tap, you can delete redundant photos,
videos, documents, and more to reclaim your valuable storage space!

*Key Features:*

- Clean Redundant Media: Scan and clean large files and old media that are no
longer needed.
- File Cleanup: Remove unnecessary documents, voice notes, and backups
cluttering your WhatsApp storage.
- Fast & Easy: Declutter storage in just a few taps with an intuitive,
user-friendly interface.
- Preview Before Deleting: Always preview media before deletion to ensure
you're removing only the files you don't need.

Note: WhatsApp Cleaner is a third-party application that helps in cleaning
files generated by WhatsApp, and is not affiliated to or endorsed by WhatsApp itself.
